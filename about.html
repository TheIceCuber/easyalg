About EasyAlg

Hello, I'm Josh, the creator of EasyAlg. I'm 14 years old and have been a dedicated cuber for nearly five years. Timing and refining my skills, particularly with 2x2 cubes, has been a major part of my cubing journey. My focus is often on learning and training algorithms, especially for CLL (Corners of the Last Layer)—one of the most widely used 2x2 algorithm sets, with about 40 unique cases.

Remembering and executing these algorithms quickly can be challenging, so consistent practice is essential. This inspired me to create EasyAlg, a specialized tool designed to help cubers like you improve your 2x2 skills. Currently, EasyAlg supports CLL algorithms, and I'm planning to add even more options soon.

Feel free to start practicing with the Algorithm Trainer or check out the How to Use page for more details. Thanks for joining me on this journey to make algorithm training more accessible and enjoyable—stay tuned for updates!
